How to contribute to Rubyest site?

All site content is accessible at github's rubyee repo Please clone it and send us merge request. We are using very nice Staticmatic generator, so please investigate it a bit before contributing.

Rubyest is open for major contributions as well, however we have one simple rule: whatever you build, please find time to support your effort in long term, because most of us are already very busy with our day job.
