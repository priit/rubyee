Rubyest

Estonia Ruby User Group

We are Ruby programming language users and enthusiasts in Estonia. Rubyest has forty programmers from fifteen companies.

Ruby is becoming popular among developers in Estonia and our goal is to help people even more to engage and exchange with ruby technology and knowledge.

We have meetups

It's a pleasure to invite you to join our meetup RubyKino. We announce each meetup date at our list.

We are open

You are welcome to join with our usergroup regardless the level of ruby you have at the moment.
You can start engaging by jonining with our official email list. Welcome!
